Roasted Duck

Vietnamese roasted duck is a flavorful dish often served during celebrations. The duck is marinated with spices like five-spice powder, garlic, and honey, then roasted until the skin is crispy and the meat tender.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole duck (about 4-5 lbs)
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp rice wine (or dry sherry)
  • 1 tbsp five-spice powder
  • 4 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 thumb ginger (minced)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper

Procedures

  1. Clean and pat dry the duck. Prick the skin lightly with a fork.
  2. Mix soy sauce, oyster sauce, honey, rice wine, five-spice, garlic, ginger, salt, and pepper into a marinade.
  3. Rub the marinade inside and outside the duck. Let it rest in the fridge for at least 4 hours (overnight for best flavor).
  4. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Place duck on a rack in a roasting pan.
  5. Roast for 1½-2 hours, basting occasionally, until skin is golden and crisp.
  6. Let rest before carving. Serve with rice or noodles.
